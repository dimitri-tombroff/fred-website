More than a Kubernetes Specialist

Fred is a more than a Kubernetes assistant. It automatically discovers and analyze your K8 applications to provide you with ready to use documentation and audit. Its interacts with you to complete its knowledge with your business use case.

Full Python

Fred is 100% python, leveraging langchain and langgraph . It is designed to be easily extended. Instead of limiting what you can do, it simply makes you start from a clear and sound design to implement your own additional expert agent.

A state-of-the art laboratory

Fred leverages langchain and langraph using state-of-the-art multi-agent patterns. No heavy or limiting framework in between.

It makes it a valuable starting point to explore multi-agent design, pros and cons. Join us to improve it.

Simple to setup

You need a Kubernetes cluster access, and your llm provider token. Fred easily runs on a laptop.

Helm charts are provided should you want to deploy it on your cluster to offer a robust and shared assistant service.
